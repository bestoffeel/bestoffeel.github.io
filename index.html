Last updated: June 21, 2026


This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you play our mobile game "Project O2S" (the "Game") and visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal identity information directly from users. However, the Game may use third-party services that collect information used to identify you.

  • Device Information: Operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Log Data: In case of an error in the Game, we collect data and information (through third-party products) on your phone called Log Data. This Log Data may include information such as your device Internet Protocol ("IP") address, device name, operating system version, the configuration of the app when utilizing our Service, the time and date of your use of the Service, and other statistics.

2. Third-Party Services

We use third-party SDKs and service providers to support gameplay, analyze performance, and serve advertisements. These third parties may collect and process data in accordance with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services: For saving user progress, cloud saves, achievements, and leaderboard services. (Privacy Policy)
  • Google AdMob: For serving relevant advertisements and monetization. AdMob collects device advertising identifiers (such as Google Advertising ID) to customize ads. (Privacy Policy)

3. Cookies

Cookies are files with a small amount of data that are commonly used as anonymous unique identifiers. These are sent to your browser from the websites that you visit and are stored on your device's internal memory. The Game does not use these "cookies" explicitly. However, the Game may use third-party code and libraries that use "cookies" to collect information and improve their services.

4. Children’s Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13 years of age. In the case we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we will be able to do the necessary actions.

5. Security

We value your trust in providing us your Information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. But remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
